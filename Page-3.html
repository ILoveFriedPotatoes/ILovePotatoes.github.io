The Baked potato

Now we shall talk about the baked potato, probably one of the best types of potatoes and most well known potato in the world, it is very simple to make and can be added to as wanted. Much like the boiled potato, this form of potato is very versitile in its flavour, and with the added bonus of having a crunch and a new flavour.You can add many more things to your baked potato like, and not limited to
  • Cheese
  • Butter
  • Bacon
  • Bacon
  • Bacon
  • Cottage Cheese
  • Cheese
  • Butter
  • Scallops
  • etc...
As you can see, the baked potato can be very versitile in its flavour.
